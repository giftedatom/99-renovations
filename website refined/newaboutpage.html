About Us

Ninety9 Renovations is committed to delivering top-tier renovation solutions. With years of experience and a passion for quality, our team transforms spaces with professionalism and precision.

Our mission is to bring your vision to life — whether it's remodeling, repairs, or full-scale renovations. We pride ourselves on attention to detail, clear communication, and customer satisfaction.

The name Ninety9 comes from a moment with our nephew Alex, who once said, "An eight is just a sideways infinity, and nine is one more than eight. So, I love you nine." That kind of love — infinite, pure, and impossible to measure — is exactly what we want our clients to feel when they walk into their newly renovated space. It's the kind of love that makes sure you know you're home.

The idea for Ninety9 began over five years ago with brothers Jonathan and Jamie, who shared a vision for a renovation business built on trust and craftsmanship. When Jonathan and his wife Katy put their heads together, they realized the time was finally right to bring that dream to life. Today, Jonathan, Katy, Jamie, and the rest of our team are proud and grateful to be turning that vision into reality — one home at a time.

We treat every project as if we were working on our own family's home. Because to us, it's not just construction — it's care.

Let's build something beautiful together.
